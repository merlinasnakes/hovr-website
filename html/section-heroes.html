HITOMI

Hitomi doesn't care about the money prize of the tournament. She is just pleased to have a place where she can kill whoever she wants legally.

MONA

Mona is a strong independent woman who is obsessed with mechanics and weapons. She drives a self assembled hover-bike equipped with a massive number of deadly guns. Mona loves her bike, so if you care about your life, you better don't touch it.

CANDIDATE ZERO

C0 is a driver droid, created with the sole purpose of winning the competition to cash back gambling money for the HOVR Corp. C0 drives a modified hover-bike to match the tournament specs.
